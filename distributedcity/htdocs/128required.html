At Distributed City, we take your privacy very seriously. That is why we require users to connect using a browser capable of communicating using strong encryption.

Your browser only supports weak encryption. It is for this reason that you are reading this page.

If you wish to read or join Distributed City, you must first upgrade to a web browser which supports 128-bit SSL Encryption. Since virtually new every browser currently offered is equipped with this type of encryption, you will most likely be able to download the newest version of your favorite browser and enter Distributed City.

Most browsers have an 'About' page which will give you the URL you need to visit in order to upgrade. Please check your browser's 'About' page and download your browser's newest version now.
