Step 1 of 3 - Choose Nym and login password

Welcome to Distributed City.

To create a new account, you must specify a Nym and login password. 'Nym' is short for pseudonym. Your Nym name will be used as your login as well as your online identity within Distributed City. It is the name that will appear on your weblog, news and forum posts, as well as being used by others to send you encrypted instant messages.

The Nym may not contain any spaces but you may use any alphanumeric characters (A-Z, 0-9) and the underscore character (_), which is the accepted substitute for a space. These restrictions are so that Nym names may be used for email addresses and OpenPGP messaging.
Enter your preferred Nym and password below. Type confirmation of your password, then press 'Register Now' to continue.
Nym Name: Nym must be a single word at least 5 characters long.
Examples of a valid Nym are: Phil_Zimmermann, aussieJim, Agent23, he1mlich, BigBob
Password: Password must be at least 8 characters long.
Password Confirmation: A good password should contain a combination of upper and lower case letters, numbers and symbols; for example:
th!s%^g00d.
