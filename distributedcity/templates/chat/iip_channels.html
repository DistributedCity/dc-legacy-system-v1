Distributed City Chat Channels (via the Invisible Internet Project)
Distributed City has reserved several channels on IIP to provide a chat space for our users. These channels roughly correspond with the DC News, Weblog, and Forum categories. Please use the appropriate channel for your desired chat topic. If you have a web client which recognizes IRC URIs, and have IIP installed on the default port 6669, you may click on a channel name to go there now.
